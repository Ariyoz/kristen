Founded 2024

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Kirste Cleaning was founded on a simple principle: every home deserves the attention of a professional. What started as a two-person operation in New York has grown into a nationwide team of 500+ vetted cleaning professionals serving 150+ cities across the USA.

Our mission is to give homeowners and businesses back their time, their peace of mind, and the joy of living in a truly clean space.

"We don't just clean homes. We create environments where life happens better."

— Kirsten, Founder & CEO
